What is body mass index (BMI)?

Here's What You Should Know

The body mass index measures a person's relative size based on their mass and height. However, BMI is used only as a screening tool and is not determinant of an individual's overall health.

You can find a person's BMI by dividing their weight (kilograms) by the square of their height (meters). Don't worry, this calculate will do the metric conversion for you!
